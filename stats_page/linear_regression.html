Linear Regression Scenario¶

A retail company wants to know if advertising spend can predict monthly sales. They pulled monthly ad spend and sales totals for a sample of stores over the past year.

Predictor (X): Monthly advertising spend (in thousands) Outcome (Y): Monthly sales revenue (in thousands)

Question: Does advertising spend significantly predict sales revenue, and if so, how much does revenue change for each additional $1,000 spent on advertising?
